Features

Built for how you actually shop

Build lists in seconds

Tap items from a rich catalog or type anything custom. Your list is ready before you leave the house.

Aisle-by-aisle routing

Your list becomes a smart store map — every item in order so you glide through without backtracking.

Edit your store layout

Drag, resize, and rename aisles until the map reflects the exact store you shop at.

Community store layouts

Browse ready-made maps shared by shoppers who've been there. Adopt one with a single tap.

Full shopping history

Every past trip saved. Reload last week's list or re-map a favorite run in a single tap.

Assign items by aisle

Sort every product into the aisle where you'll find it. Remembered per-store, forever.

Personalize everything

Pick an accent color and alternate app icon. Make Grocery Map feel like yours.

Works offline

Your lists, maps, and store layouts are always available — no internet required at the store.
