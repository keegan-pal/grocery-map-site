Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026

The short version: Grocery Map does not collect personal information, does not run ads, and does not share your data with third parties. Your shopping lists and store layouts stay on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

Who We Are

Grocery Map is developed and operated by the Grocery Map iOS team ("we," "us," or "our"). If you have questions about this policy, contact us at grocerymap.feedback@gmail.com.

Data Stored on Your Device

All of your personal grocery data is stored locally on your iPhone and backed up through Apple's standard iCloud Backup if you have it enabled:

  • Shopping lists and grocery items
  • Custom store layouts and aisle configurations
  • Shopping trip history and analytics
  • Quick Meals and staple item preferences
  • App settings and preferences

We do not have access to this data. It never passes through our servers.

iCloud CloudKit (Community Features)

When you choose to share a store layout via the Community tab, that layout, along with a display name you choose, is uploaded to Apple's CloudKit public database and becomes visible to other users. This is the only data that leaves your device, and it is shared only when you take an explicit action to publish it. Your shopping lists, history, and personal data are never shared.

Community content is associated with the display name you enter — not your real name, email, or Apple Account, unless you choose to include personal information in those fields (which we ask you not to do). You can delete content you have shared at any time from within the app.

Moderation. To keep the community safe, display names and shared layout names are automatically screened for objectionable language, and users can report content. When you report content, a record of that report (associated with your device's CloudKit identifier) is stored to power automatic hiding of flagged content. Content that crosses a report threshold is hidden automatically. See our Terms of Use for the full community content policy.

Apple's iCloud services are governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy.

Apple Reminders

Grocery Map can read from and write to Apple Reminders to let you import existing reminder lists as grocery items or export your shopping list as reminders. This access requires your explicit permission and can be revoked at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Reminders. We only access Reminders when you initiate an import or export action — we never read your Reminders in the background.

Photos

Grocery Map may access your photo library in two ways:

  • Import (Pro): You can pick a photo from your library to extract a grocery list using on-device text recognition. The selected image is processed locally and is not transmitted anywhere.
  • Save to Photos (Pro): You can save a map image of your shopping list directly to your photo library. This requires add-only access to Photos.

Photo access can be revoked at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos.

In-App Purchases

Grocery Map Pro is offered as an auto-renewable subscription through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We never receive, store, or process your payment information. For billing questions, visit support.apple.com/billing.

Analytics & Advertising

Grocery Map contains no third-party analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no tracking pixels. We do not collect crash reports, usage statistics, or behavioral data through any third-party service. We do not sell or share your personal information, and we do not engage in cross-app tracking as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

Data Retention & Deletion

Because your personal grocery data lives on your device, it is retained until you delete it or remove the app. Deleting the app removes the local data from your device (an iCloud Backup, if enabled, follows Apple's retention rules).

Community content you publish remains in CloudKit until you delete it from within the app. To remove all of your shared community content, delete each shared layout from the Community tab. If you need help removing content, contact us at grocerymap.feedback@gmail.com.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to access, correct, or delete personal information, and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising those rights.

Because Grocery Map does not collect or store your personal information on our own servers, most of your data is already under your direct control on your device. For community content stored in iCloud, you can access and delete it within the app at any time. To exercise any privacy right or ask a question, email us at grocerymap.feedback@gmail.com and we will respond as required by applicable law.

Children's Privacy

Grocery Map is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information through the community features, contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the effective date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or concerns about this privacy policy? Email us at grocerymap.feedback@gmail.com.
